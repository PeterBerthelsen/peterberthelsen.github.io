About

Food blogs are terrible.

Don't get me wrong, having a million recipes available by a simple web search is a great thing, but it's nearly impossible to find a recipe site that isn't loaded with bloat.

From ads, banners, popups, and newsletter sign-up forms to lenghty backstories that force you scroll down and risk accidentally clicking on a slow-loading ad before you can see the dang recipe you came for, finding a recipe online can be a major pain.

That's what JTDR is for. We read the ads so you don't have to! Just copy/paste a recipe URL and smash the Get Recipe button. We'll scour the website and do our best to cut through the ads, backstory, unnecessary commentary and deliver to you exactly what our name suggests: just the dang recipe.
